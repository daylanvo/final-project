Ja'Marr Chase

Home Page

Ja'Maar Chase, a 24 year old wide reciever for the Cincinnati Bengals. Chase was born in Harvey, Louisiana on March 1st 2000. During his highschool career he had 115 receptions for 2152 yards and 30 touchdowns. The four star recruit was ranked 84th overall in the nation and 15th reciever prospect. After committing to two other colleges, Chase ultimately chose to attend LSU. During his college career, Chase had a total of 107 receptions for 2093 yard and 23 touchdowns. Currently in his time in the NFL, Chase has won the Offensive Rookie of the Year, Second-Team All-Pro, and 3x at the Pro-Bowl. During the 2024 season, Chase is having an incredible season with 93 receptions for 1319 yards and 15 touchdowns in 14 weeks of regualted football.
